Family Connection Hub

Held every last Saturday of the month. A welcoming space for parents and children to connect, build confidence, and enjoy activities together.

About this programme

Family Connection Hub offers group activities, creative play, and peer support. We signpost families to relevant services and ensure a culturally sensitive, community-led environment.

  • Creative and play-based activities for parents & children
  • Peer support & signposting
  • Open to families across West Glasgow

Want to join? Contact us to check the exact time and location for the next session.
